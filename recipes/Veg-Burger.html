Veg Burger

Description

A veggie burger is a meat-free patty made from ingredients like vegetables, beans, and grains, often combined with seasonings. It offers a plant-based alternative to traditional burgers, popular among vegetarians

ingredients

  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated carrots
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Olive oil for cooking

Steps

  1. Mash the Beans: In a large bowl, mash the black beans with a fork until mostly smooth.
  2. Mix Ingredients:Add the cooked quinoa, breadcrumbs, grated carrots, chopped onion, minced garlic, soy sauce, cumin, smoked paprika, black pepper, and egg. Mix until well combined.
  3. Form Patties: Divide the mixture into 4-6 portions and shape them into patties.
  4. Cook Patties: Heat a tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook the patties for 4-5 minutes on each side, until they are golden brown and crispy.
  5. Serve: Serve the patties on buns with your favorite toppings such as lettuce, tomato, pickles, onions, and condiments like ketchup, mustard, or mayo.
